About NTX Aerial

NTX Aerial IT Consulting is led by Mark Haynie, an identity and access management specialist based in North Texas. Mark has spent his career at the intersection of security, compliance, and the way businesses actually operate — from enterprise financial-services IAM programs to hands-on engagements with clinical research organizations and family-run companies.

The philosophy is simple: most access problems aren't technology problems. They're clarity problems. Once you can see who has access to what — and why — the fixes are usually straightforward. NTX Aerial exists to bring that clarity to teams that don't have (and don't need) a full-time security department.
